Home Health Aide

We know how important it is for many seniors to continue living in their homes, and we are proud to make that possible. Most seniors find joy and meaning when they can live in the home they love as they age and remain close to their community. Comfort Loving home care provides senior care services that can help the elderly remain independent in their own homes.

We believe that an in-home care plan should be customized to each individual's needs, goals, and living situation. The best care improves quality of life by focusing on the care activities that increase engagement, improve physical and mental wellness, and bring happiness, purpose and hope.
Our services focus on physical needs and total wellbeing. We believe that everyone should experience the best of life, no matter their age or the level of care that is needed.



Our in-home care includes:

  • Personal care assistance (bathing, grooming, dressing, toileting, etc.)
  • Companionship and emotional support
  • Meal planning and preparation
  • Laundry and light housekeeping
  • Medication reminders
  • Assistance with other activities of daily living
